#1

absentee

▼
因工作、学业、岗位、职务等原因缺勤的人。
A person who is absent from his or her employment, school, post, duty, etc.
📚 讲解与辨析 (Explanation)
  • 💡 作为名词：居住在其财产所在地以外的另一个地区或国家的土地所有者。
  • 💡 作为名词：不存在或缺乏的事物。
🔗 常见搭配 (Collocations)
  • absentee landlords (缺席房东)
  • absentee ownership (缺席所有权)
  • absentee owners (缺席业主)
  • absentee landlord (缺席房东)
  • absentee ballots (缺席选票)
#2

absorbed

/æbˈsɔɹbd/
▼
包含在内，使其不再单独存在；压倒;使消失，如同被吞噬一样；合并；同化；吸收和使用。
To include so that it no longer has separate existence; to overwhelm; to cause to disappear as if by swallowing up; to incorporate; to assimilate; to take in and use up.
📚 讲解与辨析 (Explanation)
  • 💡 作为动词：吞没，如浸入水中；吞掉。
  • 💡 作为动词：吸吮；吸吮；喝进去；像海绵或身体的乳糜管一样吸收；以化学方式吸收。
近义词 (Synonyms)
  • incorporate (合并)
  • suck (吸)
  • engross (全神贯注)
  • assimilate (同化)
  • use up (用完)
反义词 (Antonyms)
  • emit (发射)
🔗 常见搭配 (Collocations)
  • absorbed into (吸收到)
  • absorbed from (吸收自)
  • absorbed through (吸收通过)
  • absorbed dose (吸收剂量)
  • absorbed all (全部吸收)
📰 真实语境 (In Context)
"Heat, light, and electricity are absorbed in the substances into which they pass."
📖 Dictionary Example

#5

abstain

/əbˈsteɪn/
▼
保留或克制自己。
Keep or withhold oneself.
📚 讲解与辨析 (Explanation)
  • 💡 作为动词：避免（某事或做某事）；避免做某事，尤其是放纵自己的行为。
  • 💡 作为动词：快（一段时间内不吃东西）。
近义词 (Synonyms)
  • deny (否认)
  • refrain (克制)
  • withhold (保留)
  • forbear (隐忍)
  • relinquish (放弃)
🔗 常见搭配 (Collocations)
  • abstain from (放弃)
  • abstain altogether (完全弃权)
  • abstain entirely (完全弃权)
  • abstain completely (完全弃权)
  • abstain when (弃权时)
📰 真实语境 (In Context)
"In order to improve his health, Rob decided to abstain from smoking."
