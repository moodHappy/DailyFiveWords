#3

walking

▼
n. 步行；散步；v. 步行（walk 的 ing 形式）；adj. 步行的
To move on the feet by alternately setting each foot (or pair or group of feet, in the case of animals with four or more feet) forward, with at least one foot on the ground at all times. Compare run.
📚 讲解与辨析 (Explanation)
  • 💡 作为动词：“自由行走”，即赢得或避免刑事法庭案件，特别是当确实有罪时。
  • 💡 作为动词：物体丢失或被盗。
近义词 (Synonyms)
  • pinched (捏)
  • waulk (沃克)
  • acquitted (无罪释放)
  • full (完整)
  • get off (下车)
反义词 (Antonyms)
  • run (跑步)
🔗 常见搭配 (Collocations)
  • walking distance (步行距离)
  • walking beam (n. （蒸汽机的）步进梁；活动梁)
  • within walking distance (步行就能到)
  • walking stick (n. 手杖)
  • walking street (步行街)
📰 真实语境 (In Context)
"To walk briskly for an hour every day is to keep fit."

#5

shorten

▼
vt. 缩短；减少；变短；vi. 缩短；变短
To make shorter; to abbreviate.
📚 讲解与辨析 (Explanation)
  • 💡 作为动词：变短。
  • 💡 作为动词：使有缺陷（至）；剥夺（的）。
反义词 (Antonyms)
  • lengthen (延长)
  • extend (延长)
🔗 常见搭配 (Collocations)
  • shorten sail (收帆减速)
  • curtail or shorten (截短或缩短)
  • shorten cyclic codes (缩短循环码)
  • Accumulation shorten (缺乏积累)
  • shorten a ladder (缩合梯子)
📰 真实语境 (In Context)
"to shorten an allowance of food"
