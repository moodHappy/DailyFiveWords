#1

clique

▼
n. 派系；阀；私党；小圈子；vi. 结党；n. （Clique）人名；（法）克利克
A small, exclusive group of individuals, usually according to lifestyle or social status; a cabal.
📚 讲解与辨析 (Explanation)
  • 💡 作为名词：与完全图同构的子图。
  • 💡 作为名词：一组相互链接的相关网站，就像网络环，但具有由派系所有者决定的独家成员资格。
近义词 (Synonyms)
  • ingroup (组内)
  • coterie (小圈子)
  • camp (营地)
  • inner circle (内圈)
🔗 常见搭配 (Collocations)
  • clique problem (分团问题)
  • New Guangxi clique (新桂系)
  • The Clique (女校风波)
  • Anhui clique (皖系军阀)
  • clique graph (团图)
📰 真实语境 (In Context)
"This school used to be really friendly, but now everyone keeps to their own cliques."

#2

resilient

▼
adj. （人或动物）对困境有承受力的，有复原力的；（物质或物体）有弹性的，有回弹力的
(of objects or substances) Returning quickly to original shape after force is applied; elastic.
📚 讲解与辨析 (Explanation)
  • 💡 作为形容词：（系统的有机体或人）在破坏性事件或条件后迅速恢复正常。
近义词 (Synonyms)
  • strong (强)
  • bendable (可弯曲)
  • flexible (灵活的)
反义词 (Antonyms)
  • fragile (脆弱)
  • brittle (脆)
🔗 常见搭配 (Collocations)
  • Be Resilient About Problems (坚韧乐观地面对难题)
  • Resilient Packet Ring (技术)
  • resilient flooring (弹性地板)
  • resilient mounting (弹性机垫)
  • resilient coupling (弹性联轴器)

#3

detonate

▼
vt. 使爆炸；vi. 引爆，爆炸
To explode; to blow up. Specifically, to combust supersonically via shock compression.
📚 讲解与辨析 (Explanation)
  • 💡 作为动词：导致爆炸。
近义词 (Synonyms)
  • discharge (放电)
  • blast (爆炸)
  • fulminate (暴发)
反义词 (Antonyms)
  • deflagrate (爆燃)
🔗 常见搭配 (Collocations)
  • Detonate Corpse (尸体爆裂)
  • Detonate Healing Turret (引爆治疗炮台)
  • Ice Wall Detonate (冰墙爆炸)
  • Detonate Mine Field (引爆雷区)

#4

revolting

▼
adj. 叛乱的，背叛的；令人讨厌的，使人厌恶的；v. 反叛，造反；使反感（revolt 的现在分词）
To rebel, particularly against authority.
📚 讲解与辨析 (Explanation)
  • 💡 作为动词：极大地排斥。
  • 💡 作为动词：导致回头；滚动或向后行驶；飞行。
🔗 常见搭配 (Collocations)
  • in revolt (反抗；造反)
  • revolt against (反感；厌恶)
  • revolt at (厌恶；憎恶)
📰 真实语境 (In Context)
"The farmers had to revolt against the government to get what they deserved."
