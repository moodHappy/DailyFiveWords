#1

testosterone

▼
n. [生化] 睾酮，睾丸素（男性荷尔蒙的一种）
Steroid hormone produced primarily in the testes of the male; it is responsible for the development of secondary sex characteristics in the male.
📚 讲解与辨析 (Explanation)
  • 💡 作为名词：男子气概的行为，通常具有攻击性或愚蠢鲁莽的性质。
🔗 常见搭配 (Collocations)
  • Testosterone Propionate (丙酸睾酮)
  • testosterone enanthate (庚酸睾酮)
  • Testosterone Undecanoate (十一酸睾酮)
  • Testosterone acetate (醋酸睾酮)
  • Testosterone decanoate (癸酸睾酮)
📰 真实语境 (In Context)
"Mother encouraged James to rely more on intelligence and less on testosterone to deal with the neighbor's son."

#2

emerald

▼
n. 翡翠，绿宝石；翠绿色；尺娥；蜻蛉，豆娘；一种小型蜂鸟（羽亮绿色，翼尾色深，主要产于加勒比地区和中美洲）；adj. 翠绿色的，鲜绿色的；【名】 （Emerald）（美、俄、印、澳、马）埃默拉尔德（人名）
Any of various green gemstones, especially a green transparent form of beryl, highly valued as a precious stone.
📚 讲解与辨析 (Explanation)
  • 💡 作为名词：翠绿，一种颜色。
  • 💡 作为名词：Chlorostilbon 和 Elvira 属的任何蜂鸟；还有一些属于阿玛齐利亚属
近义词 (Synonyms)
  • minionette (小黄人)
  • smaragd (斯玛拉格德)
🔗 常见搭配 (Collocations)
  • emerald green (鲜绿色)

#3

clasp

▼
n. 扣子，扣环；紧握，紧抱；v. 扣住，扣紧；握紧，抱紧；紧紧缠绕
A fastener or holder, particularly one that clasps.
📚 讲解与辨析 (Explanation)
  • 💡 作为名词：（单数）拥抱、抓握或握手。
  • 💡 作为动词：抓住；抓住；掌握；紧紧地抓住。
近义词 (Synonyms)
  • join (加入)
  • put together (放在一起)
  • grip (抓地力)
  • begrip (抓住)
  • attach (附)
🔗 常见搭配 (Collocations)
  • collar clasp (领钩)
  • clasp arm (卡环臂)
  • clasp denture (卡环托牙)
  • Stainless Steel Clasp (不锈钢丝卡环)
  • clasp guideline (带钩导线)
📰 真实语境 (In Context)
"I always have a hard time working the clasp on this necklace!"

#5

extinguish

▼
v. 熄灭；消灭，使破灭；<法律>取消（权利，义务），使无效；偿清；压制
To put out, as in fire; to end burning; to quench
📚 讲解与辨析 (Explanation)
  • 💡 作为动词：摧毁或废除某物
  • 💡 作为动词：使某事变得模糊或黯淡
近义词 (Synonyms)
  • douse (浇灭)
  • put out (放出)
  • quench (淬火)
🔗 常见搭配 (Collocations)
  • arc extinguish chamber (灭弧室)
  • EXTINGUISH FIRE (灭火)
  • extinguish them (消灭他们)
  • arc extinguish device (灭弧器)
  • self-extinguish ability (自熄性)
📰 真实语境 (In Context)
"She extinguished all my hopes."
